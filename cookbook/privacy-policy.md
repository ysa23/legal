# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-21_

This is the privacy policy for **Cookbook** (the "app"), published by
Yosef Shmueli. If anything is unclear, email `cookbook@house-of-code.com`.

## What we collect

Nothing. At no time is any of your content sent to or obtained by us.
We do not collect recipes, personal information, analytics, or any
other data.

## Where your data lives

Your recipes are stored on your device. If you choose to use a cloud
sync service (such as iCloud on iOS or Google account sync on Android),
your data is processed by that service under its own privacy policy —
not ours. We have no access to it.

## Third parties

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Deleting your data

Delete the app to remove local data. If you enabled cloud sync, remove
the app's data through your cloud account settings.

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no
data, there is nothing for us to act on — but if you have concerns,
email us.

## Changes

If we change this policy in a way that materially affects your privacy,
we'll notify you in the app before the change takes effect.

## Contact
